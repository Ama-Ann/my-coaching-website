About Me

I'm Hai Pham. After more than two decades leading finance and tax functions for some of the largest companies operating in Vietnam, I now bring that same rigor, structure, and calm under pressure to coaching — helping individuals find clarity and executives lead with confidence.

My approach

I believe coaching works best when it's practical: clear goals, honest conversations, and accountability that actually moves you forward — not just talk.
